Concept,What It Is,Purpose,State/Persistence
1. Cart (Shopping Cart),A temporary collection of products a customer intends to buy.,"To allow the customer to gather, review, and modify potential purchases before committing.","Temporary and Volatile (Can be abandoned; may be saved between sessions if the user is logged in, but is not a financial record.)"
2. Checkout,The process (typically a multi-step flow) the customer goes through after leaving the cart.,"To collect all necessary information: shipping address, billing details, shipping method, and payment information.",Process/Stage (It's the action of moving from the cart to placing the order.)
3. Order,"A persistent, official record of a completed purchase transaction.",To serve as the legal and financial commitment between the customer and the seller. It's what the seller must fulfill.,Permanent and Persistent (Created after payment is confirmed; cannot be modified by the customer in the same way a cart can.)
4. Order Items (or Line Items),The individual products and quantities that are part of a completed Order.,"To detail exactly what was purchased within a specific Order, along with the price at the moment of purchase.",Permanent (These records are linked directly to the Order and are part of the final financial record.)
